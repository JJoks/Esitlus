Founders and Coders

We are a membership-based CIC dedicated to building a more equitable economy by training software engineers and incubating businesses founded by our members.

Our values

Cooperative

Support for cooperative learning, cooperatively-owned businesses, and collaboration with the local community

Inclusive

Getting a more diverse group of people into tech training and into tech employment

Social Impact

Helping charities and nonprofits access the fruits of the digital economy

Our peer-led approach to training software engineers

Complete beginners are mentored by our full-time students during part-time study,

Students are mentored by recent graduates during our 12-week foundation programme,

They then work on projects for nonprofits with mentorship from more experienced alumni
