Pair Programming Recap

The Driving Test

  • Quick recap of last week's pair programming pointers
  • In this method, the student at the keyboard should follow the instructions of their partner
  • They can alternate roles when completing each FreeCodeCamp challenge

The backseat driver

  • Verbalise your thinking
  • Explain your reasoning
  • As the backseat driver, this is a chance to verbalise your thinking, convey it effectively, and explain your decisions to your partner. It really helps you to think about the code you‚Äôre dictating, as well as cementing the concepts in your head!

The frontseat driver

  • Follow the instructions of your partner
  • Ask for clarification
  • Whoever‚Äôs in the front seat (typing) should provide no advice, but can request clarification or an explanation of why they‚Äôre being asked to do something
  • this is a great opportunity to follow someone‚Äôs thinking really closely, yet still getting to type it out yourself

Tips for both

  • Discuss things together out loud
  • Refer to line numbers
  • Be patient!
