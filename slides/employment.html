What to look for in an employer

Culture

We look for employer parters who value their culture. We want you to join environments that nuture your development
and make you feel naturally motivated and passionate about what you're doing.

Tech stack

During the programme you'll learn best practices of software development. You can decide what it is you wish to do after the course: stick with JavaScript or learn something new.

Clients or product

Have a think about what you'd be interested in, whether it's working with clients or working on a product. Make sure you're interested in the work.

Flexibility

Flexibilty isn't something I'd recommend to look for straight away. It's more important to be around people you can learn from as much as possible.

Remuneration

Understand your value. You're entering a career in tech. If you work hard, the money will find you.
