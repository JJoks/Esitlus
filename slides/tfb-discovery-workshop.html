Tech for Better

A programme for social impact startups and nonprofits to learn how to design, test and build new digital services, working with developers in London and Palestine, and supported by Intersticia.

How will this programme benefit me?

It will give you experience of working with an agile development team,

and create a real digital service you can test with users

What is the total time commitment required of me?

Around 20 hours spread over several weeks

User Research


Some tips

Ask early

And make sure your users are within reach

Test your assumptions

This is the best time to do it!

Find users that fit your persona(s)...

But make sure you survey a diverse range of people

Ask open-ended questions

It'll help users give more information. See if they can draw upon specific incidents in their past.

Listen, don't talk

The survey questions should be seen as more of a prompt...

... and take notes!
